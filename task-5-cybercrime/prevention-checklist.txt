Stay Safe Online Checklist -

- Enable 2FA Everywhere: Turn ON 2FA for all your accounts
- Check App Permissions: Periodically check which apps have access to your Contacts and SMS
- Use secure WiFi for Login: Never log-in to portals using Public WiFi
- Hover over Links: Before clicking a link, hover your mouse over it to see the URL
- Check for HTTPS: Only enter your details on websites that start with https://
- Secure your UPI Identity: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money and in-front of anyone
- Verify UPI Beneficiaries: Always double-check the name that appears in your payment app before paying

Cybercrime Report Guide -

If you or a peer fall victim to a digital scam, speed is critical,
Immediate Action: Call the National Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 instantly to freeze fraudulent transactions or report the website.
Official Filing: Log your complaint at the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal: cybercrime.gov.in.
